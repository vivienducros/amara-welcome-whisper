

# AMARA — Regenerative Village Landing Page & Lead Quiz

A poetic, mobile-first landing page that feels like opening a luxury lifestyle magazine. Warm, minimal, emotional — designed to make visitors *feel* before they think.

---

## Brand & Design Foundation
- **Background**: Warm beige (#FFFCF8)
- **Text**: Soft black (#272727)
- **Accent/CTA**: Warm brown (#BE9071)
- **Font**: Glacial Indifference everywhere
- **Style**: Luxury editorial magazine — generous whitespace, large poetic typography, smooth transitions between sections

---

## Page Flow (Single-Page Quiz Funnel)

### Screen 1 — Welcome
- **Headline**: "Wake Up to Nature, Work With Purpose, Live Among Visionaries — Discover Your New Life in Portugal"
- **Subheadline**: "Morning light through forest. Creative work that flows. Neighbors building the future."
- **CTA Button**: "Discover Your Place" (warm brown)
- Full-screen feel, breathing space, elegant and inviting

### Screen 2 — Quiz Question 1
"What matters most to you right now?"
- A deeper connection to nature
- A community of like-minded people
- A healthier environment for my family
- A place that fuels my creativity & work

### Screen 3 — Quiz Question 2
"What best describes you?"
- Entrepreneur / Founder
- Artist / Creative
- Family looking for a new chapter
- Professional seeking more meaning

### Screen 4 — Quiz Question 3
"What's your vision for Amara?"
- I want to live there full-time or part of the year
- I want a home for my family to grow into
- I want to create and work from an inspiring place
- I'm mainly looking for a real estate investment

### Screen 5 — Quiz Question 4
"What stage of life are you in?"
- I'm in my 20s-30s, building my path
- I'm in my 30s-40s, ready for a meaningful shift
- I'm in my 40s-50s, designing my next chapter
- I'm 60+, looking for a peaceful place to enjoy life

### Screen 6 — Quiz Question 5
"Are you in a position to invest in a home within the next 12 months?"
- Yes, I have savings or assets ready (50K€+)
- I'm working toward it and could be ready soon
- Not yet, but I'm exploring for the future
- I'd prefer to start with a rental or smaller commitment

### Screen 7 — Contact Info
Three fields on one screen:
- **First name** (required)
- **Email** (required)
- **Phone number** with country code dropdown (flag + code like +351, +33, +44, +1) — auto-detects user's country as default
- Submit button in warm brown

### Screen 8 — Thank You
A warm, poetic confirmation. Something that leaves them feeling seen and excited — not a generic "thanks for submitting."

---

## Interaction & UX Details
- **One question per screen** with smooth fade/slide transitions
- **Progress indicator** — subtle dots or thin bar showing quiz progress
- **Single-tap answers** — clicking an option auto-advances to the next step (no extra "Next" button needed on quiz screens)
- **Back navigation** — subtle back arrow to revisit previous questions
- **Mobile-first layout** — optimized for phones, scales beautifully to desktop
- **No backend** — all data stays client-side, thank you page shown on completion
- **Fast loading** — lightweight page, Glacial Indifference loaded efficiently
